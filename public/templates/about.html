About

Wiser is a brand new way of learning. Online learning should be as fun and engaging as the internet. It should be open to all, not just concerning basic access, but also in learning method. There is no “one size fits all”.
We give you immediate access to inspiring and relevant pins, no week plans - no waiting - no need for prerequisites.

At Wiser we believe in different ways of learning: Some like videos, some like reading. Some need time to digest, some do it all at once. Therefore we don’t restrict - we do it all! At Wiser, Yes is more.

This version of Wiser is currently in beta and focused exclusively on Design Thinking, but it’ll soon grow to include more topics, more knowledge, more users and more fun.

If you need help to navigate Wiser, have a suggestion or just want to say hi, reach us at wiser@idadesign.dk.
